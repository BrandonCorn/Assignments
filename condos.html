The Condos at Destin Trails

What is a condo?
Our luxury condos are permanent strucutres four feet off the ground. Each condo has canvas walls, wooden floors, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the condos furnished?
Each condo is furnished with a queen-sized bed, large-screen TV, and mini-fridge. Your luxury experience includes a private bathroom with a waterfall shower and whirpool tub for two.
What should I bring?
Bathing suits, beach attire, and sunscreen.
